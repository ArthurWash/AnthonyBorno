About me

“I specialize in patience” is more than just a slogan I throw around, after years of work in various photography studios and companies throughout NJ/PA I have just about worked under every type of pressure imaginable, no group too wild or idea too difficult for me to consider! I specialize in putting your visual goal within reach and getting that memory captured!

My specialties are kids and family but I also specialize in infant, maternity, costume, headshots, communion and graduation! I shoot both outdoors and at my residential studio in Willow Grove PA, I travel with my equipment and can even set up right in your living room!

I can be reached at Bornophoto@gmail.com for all questions and needs, Can’t wait to work with you!
